Under Weight

Ensure you consume a well-balanced diet that includes a mix of proteins, carbohydrates, healthy fats, vitamins, and minerals. For additional guidance, consult with a nutritionist.

Normal Weight

For maintaining a normal weight, focus on balanced nutrition, incorporating a variety of foods in appropriate portions. Additionally, consider regular physical activity to support overall health. For additional guidance, consult with a nutritionist.

Over Weight

For those with a little excess weight, aim for a balanced diet with controlled portions. Incorporate regular exercise to promote gradual and sustainable weight management. For additional guidance, consult with a nutritionist.

Obese

For obesity, prioritize a comprehensive approach: adopt a balanced diet, engage in regular exercise, and consult a healthcare professional for personalized guidance on weight management strategies. For additional guidance, consult with a nutritionist.
